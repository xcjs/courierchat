About CourierChat

Who Are We?

We are Zackary Lowery and Kyle Moses, a pair of web developers from central Ohio who wanted to do something cool and maybe a little counter-culture.

Our Inspiration

The goal was to create a chat service that cured us of the nostalgia of signing into a chat service in the earlier days of the web.

Many times these services only required a unique alias at the time it was being claimed. Naturally, this could result in a certain "wild west" atmosphere.

In embracing this semi-anonymous approach, we hope to provide a throwback to the younger days of the web when your identity and privacy was yours to keep.

How Does CourierChat Work?

CourierChat was written to support keeping your identity hidden as much as possible.

This does not mean CourierChat is perfectly anonymous, however.

Any information you reveal about yourself will be shared with other active users in the room you join, and your IP address, which is tracked by the server during your session, can reveal your location to the administrator of the server (we do not share it willingly with third parties).

CourierChat is a centralized chat service, which means your messages are sent to our server and then dispersed to other members of your chat room.

Once these messages have been delivered to every participant, they are discarded. We act as a courier to only deliver your messages, hence our name. No tracking cookies are used, we do not target ads to our audience, and we do not sell or share your information.
